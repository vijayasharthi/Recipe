HOW TO MAKE THE VADA PAAV AND THEIR INGREDIENTS LISTED ARE

To make Vada Pav, you'll need the following ingredients:

Ingredients for Vada (Potato Filling):

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, boiled, peeled, and mashed
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of as a foetida (hing)
  • A few curry leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Coriander leaves, finely chopped (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)

    • Ingredients for Batter:

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • A pinch of turmeric powder
  • A pinch of baking soda (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (to make a smooth batter)

    • Other Ingredients:

  • Pav (soft bread rolls)
  • Green chutney (made from coriander and mint leaves)
  • Tamarind chutney (sweet and tangy)
  • Garlic chutney (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Dry garlic chutney (optional, for extra spice)
  • Oil for deep frying

    • Instructions:

    Prepare the Potato Filling (Vada):

  • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped curry leaves. Sauté for another minute.
  • Add mashed potatoes, salt, and chopped coriander leaves (if using). Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add lemon juice if desired.
  • Remove from heat and let the mixture cool down a bit.
  • Divide the potato mixture into equal portions and shape them into small round balls. Set aside.

    • Prepare the Batter:

  • In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, turmeric powder, baking soda (if using), and salt.
  • Gradually add water to the flour mixture and whisk until you get a smooth batter without any lumps. The batter should have a medium consistency, thick enough to coat the potato balls.

    • Assemble and Fry the Vadas:

  • Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai over medium heat.
  • Dip each potato ball into the batter, ensuring it's coated evenly.
  • Carefully drop the coated potato ball into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  • Remove the fried vadas from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

    • Assemble the Vada Pav:

  • Slice the pav horizontally, without cutting all the way through.
  • Spread green chutney and tamarind chutney on the inner sides of the pav.
  • Place a hot vada between the pav slices.
  • Optionally, you can also spread garlic chutney or sprinkle dry garlic chutney on top of the vada for extra flavor.
  • Serve hot with more chutneys on the side.

    • Enjoy your homemade Vada Pav, a beloved street food snack from Maharashtra!
